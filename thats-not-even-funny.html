That's Not Even Funny

DA Dhun Aggarwal · July 1, 2026
"I bawled my eyes out laughing — at myself."

Before we dive into this I really want to thank all of you for taking the time to check this out — and a disclaimer before: nothing here is written by AI (even the editing) so if there is any mistake, it is my fault. Hope you like it :)

It clicked for me during a roast. I told a friend his coding was so bad that even Stack Overflow had given up on him. That was good, right? He fired back that my sense of humour was like a pop quiz: noone asked for it, everyone hated it and it lowered the room's average.

Okay. That's just genius. I mean the roast is still about me but, you can't help appreciating art. I bawled my eyes out of laughing on the "lowered the room's average" part.

We're all somehow trained to take ourselves seriously, to present a clean, unbothered version of us, that's good in fact but I have quietly opted out. I am the person who walks in a room, trips over nothing and turns it into a bit.

If you're reading this and thinking What is wrong with this maniac? Fair. But congratulations, you're in the perfect place because that's the lens we're changing — and if not, congratulations, you're gonna love this.

Either way, pull up a chair. It's about to get ridiculous.
